introduction

It gives us immense pleasure to introduce ourselves as, India’s largest export house in ready-to-wear garments for men and women. It has been over 3 decades that we have  been exporting our garments across the globe, namely countries like Japan, South America, Australia and Europe. We are an export-oriented garment company with an annual turnover of more than US $ 20 million. Our sampling unit and our corporate office is at MIDC, Andheri (E), near the international airport in Mumbai, also we are having Sea Port for your shipment in time. We grow global brands,  Lady London leverages a diversified portfolio of brands including Zara , Born in Australia, one of the largest wholesalers in Japan. Ensuring that the quality is unaffected due to mass production, we take pride in our craftsmanship and timely shipment/Delivery with best quality garment. Fabric we use 100% Cotton, 100% Rayon & Linen Garments. Also we can make garment in 100% Cotton Yarn Dyed Fabric any type of Yarn Dyed. We can make like Moss Crepe, Linen & Other Garments.
